Notes

  • The names on the left are Intel/NEC names, the names on the right are Intel 8080/Zilog Z80 names, and - if it is unavailable
  • The 8080/Z80 SP register corresponds conceptually to the 8086 SP register. However, in V20 8080 emulation mode, SP is reserved for system interrupts (which are handled in 8086 native mode) and BP serves the purpose of the 8080/Z80 SP registers.
  • The identification between the Z80 IX/IY registers and the 8086 SI/DI registers has been verified for the NEC ÂµPD9002 emulation mode.
  • The Z80 I and R registers have similar functionality to the 80286 IDTR and Pentium TSC registers, respectively.
